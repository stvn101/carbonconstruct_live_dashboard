# CarbonConstruct Landing Page Funnel Strategy

## Objective
Create a high-converting landing page that captures emails and keeps visitors engaged through a strategic funnel of value propositions and CTAs.

## Funnel Design

### Stage 1: Hero Section (Primary Conversion)
- **Goal:** Immediate email capture
- **Elements:**
  - Bold headline emphasizing carbon savings accountability
  - Subheading with social proof (e.g., "85 active projects, 1,240t CO2e saved")
  - Primary CTA: "Get Started Free" (email form)
  - Visual: Hero image or animated background showing construction + green energy

### Stage 2: Value Proposition (Engagement)
- **Goal:** Build trust and interest
- **Elements:**
  - 3 key benefits with icons
  - Real-time dashboard preview (embed the live dashboard)
  - Testimonials or case studies

### Stage 3: Features & Social Proof (Secondary Conversion)
- **Goal:** Deepen engagement, secondary email capture
- **Elements:**
  - Feature cards with hover effects
  - Live activity feed showing real projects
  - Industry stats and certifications

### Stage 4: Call-to-Action Section (Tertiary Conversion)
- **Goal:** Final push for email or demo booking
- **Elements:**
  - "See It Live" CTA (links to live dashboard)
  - "Request Demo" CTA (email capture)
  - FAQ section

### Stage 5: Footer (Retention)
- **Goal:** Newsletter signup and trust signals
- **Elements:**
  - Email newsletter signup
  - Links to social proof
  - Trust badges

## Design Philosophy
- **Color Scheme:** Dark background with neon green accents (consistent with dashboard)
- **Typography:** Space Grotesk for headlines, Inter for body
- **Animations:** Smooth scroll reveals, hover effects, subtle parallax
- **Glassmorphism:** Consistent with dashboard aesthetic
- **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first design

## Conversion Strategy
1. **Multiple Entry Points:** Email capture at hero, mid-page, and footer
2. **Progressive Disclosure:** Show more value as user scrolls
3. **Social Proof:** Real data from live dashboard
4. **Urgency:** Limited-time offer or "Join 85+ projects"
5. **Friction Reduction:** Simple forms, clear CTAs
